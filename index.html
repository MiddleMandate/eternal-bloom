The Core Idea

What is UFT-EB?

The Unified Field Theory of Eternal Bloom (UFT-EB) proposes a single organizing principle for three of physics' deepest questions: when does quantum superposition give way to classical reality? How does gravity emerge from quantum degrees of freedom? And why does time have a preferred direction?

The answer, according to UFT-EB: classical reality emerges when internal informational coherence crosses a critical, observer-independent threshold. This threshold is calculated from the system's own entropy, mutual information, and decoherence state. No external observer is needed. No randomness is required. The system itself determines when the quantum dream ends and classical certainty begins.

Deterministic Collapse

Unlike stochastic models (GRW, CSL), UFT-EB collapse is determined entirely by the system's informational state. Given complete knowledge of the density matrix and entropy, the collapse point is calculable.

Observer Independence

The collapse threshold is defined in terms of intrinsic system properties. No external observer, measurement apparatus, or conscious agent is required to precipitate the quantum-to-classical transition.

Gravity from Information

Through the entropy-curvature coupling, the same informational processes that drive collapse also source spacetime curvature. General relativity emerges as the classical limit of informational dynamics.
